* Amplify left or right channel

If you have digitized a cassette and want to amplify or attenuate one channel only, this plug-in will do it.

Variables

1. Channel selector: 0=left channel, 1=right channel (default 0).
2. Volume to amplify or attenuate the channel (default 0 db, no change in volume).

Written specifically for people who use a screen reader, and people who prefer to use the keyboard over a mouse.
