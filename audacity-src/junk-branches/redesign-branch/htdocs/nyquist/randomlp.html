Random Low pass Filter

Like someone is playing around with the cutoff frequency knob of your low pass filter. As with Random Panning and Random Amp Modulation, there are maxspeed and factor controls, plus a third control, the maximum cutoff frequency of the low pass filter.

Once again, the lower you set maxspeed, the higher you must set factor to get similar depth of random filtering result. You can use factor to decrease or increase the amount of randomness modulation.

If you generate white noise then apply this effect, you can _partially_ simulate wind sound. the rising and falling tones of wind are for another plug-in...
