* Time Shift Tool

A plug-in for performing the same task as the time shift tool in Audacity.

Choose the track you want to time shift (0=left, 1=right), and the amount to shift (range from 0.0 to 100.0 milliseconds). If you choose the left channel shifted by 10.0ms, it will start 10.0ms later than previously.

Only works on stereo audio. Useful for aligning tracks due to recording latency. Also can be used for stereo effects.
