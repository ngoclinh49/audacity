Random amplitude modulation

Similar to Random Panning, this time playing around with the volume knob. You can set the maximum speed (maxspeed, in hz) of the random changes. The other setting, factor, determines how deeply the random amp effect is. Because of the way the random signal is generated, the lower maxspeed is, the higher factor must be in order to produce the same amp result. (Factor can be used to increase or decrease the amp effect.)
