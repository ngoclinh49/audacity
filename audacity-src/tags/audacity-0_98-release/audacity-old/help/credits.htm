Credits

Note: If you have questions about Audacity, please don't email the individual developers. Instead, send your mail to audacity-help@lists.sourceforge.net. Thanks!

Dominic Mazzoni is the project leader and Audacity's primary programmer. He began the project while a graduate student at Carnegie Mellon University in Pittsburgh, PA. He is currently a research programmer at the NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory in Pasadena, CA.
dominic@minorninth.com

Roger Dannenberg wrote the sound file I/O, MIDI file manipulation, and real-time audio I/O code for Audacity. He is a Senior Research Scientist at Carnegie Mellon University in Pittsburgh, PA.
rbd@cs.cmu.edu

Joshua Haberman wrote the Preferences dialog and maintains the distributions for Windows, FreeBSD, and Debian Linux. He is a student at the University of Puget Sound in Seattle, WA.
joshua@haberman.com

Nasca Octavian Paul wrote many of the digital effects included in Audacity, including BassBoost, Phaser, and Wahwah. He studies Mathematics and Computer Science at Petru Maior University in Targu-Mures, Romania.
pnasca@science.uttgm.ro

Thanks to Harvey Lubin of Agrapha for the logo and icons.

Thanks to Tom Woodhams for the Aqua toolbar for the Macintosh

Thanks to Tony Oetzmann for the tutorials, and much of the design of the online help.
