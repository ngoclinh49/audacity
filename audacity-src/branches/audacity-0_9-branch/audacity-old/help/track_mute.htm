Track Mute Button

Clicking on this button will mute this track , i.e. this track is switched off without deleting it. This action is not reversible with the Undo function, however you can un-mute a track just by clicking the mute button again.

Muted tracks are easy to spot, because their Mute buttons are green.

To unmute the track, click on the button again. (button turns grey again)

The state of the mute buttons is NOT saved in the project file, and it does not affect mixing (i.e. muted tracks are still mixed when you export or Quick Mix).
